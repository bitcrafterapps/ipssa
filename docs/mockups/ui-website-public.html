FOR HOMEOWNERS

How to find and hire an IPSSA professional

All IPSSA members are part of a local chapter, peer-rated by other professionals, and trained to the same association standard.

1

Search by ZIP code

Enter your ZIP or city and browse verified IPSSA members in your area.

2

Compare professionals

Review CoverageScores, peer ratings, certifications, and service specialties side by side.

3

Contact & hire

Request a quote directly. All members are association-backed and chapter-accountable.

4

Rate your experience

Your feedback improves the quality of the IPSSA network for all homeowners.

What does IPSSA membership mean?

IPSSA is an independent trade association that requires members to pass certification exams, follow a code of ethics, and remain accountable through chapter standing rules.

What is Tech-4-Tech route coverage?

If your IPSSA professional is sick or injured, the chapter's Tech-4-Tech program ensures another verified member covers your pool. Your service doesn't stop.

How are CoverageScores calculated?

CoverageScores are peer ratings from other IPSSA members — not just homeowners. They measure service quality, communication, and professionalism across coverage jobs.

Is there a cost to search for a professional?

No. Searching for and contacting IPSSA pool professionals is completely free for homeowners. Membership fees apply only to pool service professionals joining IPSSA.
