High CYA pools — the partial drain trick

For pools stuck above 100 ppm CYA, I do a 20–30% partial drain and refill before adding any oxidizer. Cut my chemical costs by ~30% on these accounts and the chlorine holds way better after.

Salt cell cleaning frequency

Inspect cells every 3 months in SD — our hard water calcifies faster than the manual says. Dilute muriatic acid 4:1 water, 10-minute soak max. Never exceed 15 min.
