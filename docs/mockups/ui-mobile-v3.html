What is the ideal free chlorine range for a residential swimming pool?

Why 1.0 – 3.0 ppm?

This range provides effective sanitation without irritating swimmers. Below 1.0 ppm, chlorine may not kill pathogens fast enough. Above 3.0 ppm can cause skin and eye irritation. Always test before and after adding chemicals.
